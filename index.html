My name is Lena Harris and I am a graduate student at the University of Rochester . My research is in environmental economics with a focus on water scarcity. In addition to my individual work, I work at the intersection of health and environmental economics with the HEEL Lab at Rochester. Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, gardening, and crocheting.
